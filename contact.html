License Management

UCOS software may be downloaded to any compatible computer. In order to operate the software, however, a valid license key is required. The key is unique to each software/hardware combination.

An updated key is required to make changes such as moving the software to a new computer, upgrading an existing license, transferring a license or removing a computer (blacklisting a license.)

Contact UCOS Support to obtain or change licenses.
